Due to the OpenPose having a high level of accuracy, it also requires a high performing GPU to handle it, which would require a PC. It does not seem feesible to put a full PC in the makerlab connected to a webcam, so I need a better solution which reduces the physical space taken in the lab. The best solution is to setup a Raspberry Pi connected to a Pi-Cam in the lab and stream the videoa live to a PC for processing. The Raspberry Pi has been chosen for this because it is a small microprocessor that takes up a minimum amount of space in your lab.
